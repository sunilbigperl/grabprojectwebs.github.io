CMS Website

So what exactly is a CMS website?

A CMS or a 'Content Management System' quite literally allows you to control and manage the content within your website - without technical training. Using this uncomplicated system you can very easily add, delete images and edit text in your website on the fly. You can also have an unlimited number of pages and a full site-search engine. What's more... with 'Navega Bem - Web Design' you can have a highly professional CMS website at a very reasonable price indeed!
